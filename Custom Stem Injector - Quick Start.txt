Custom Stem Injector - Quick Start Guide

Recommended Workflow (2-Stem)

1. Open the app.
2. Select 2 stems mode.
3. Select your base audio file.
4. Click Step 1: Extract 2 Stems (Kim-2).
5. Wait until extraction completes and the 2-stem fields auto-fill.
6. Click Step 2: Prepare Serato Files.
7. Click Step 3: Build Stem File.

After Build Completes

1. Open the prepared output folder shown by the app.
2. Locate the original song file and the matching .serato-stems file.
3. Keep both files together in the same folder.
4. Move both files to your desired music/library location.
5. Add the song to Serato.

Important Notes

- Start in 2-stem mode unless you specifically need 4-stem inputs.
- Do not separate the song file from its .serato-stems sidecar.
- If moving files later, always move the song and .serato-stems together.
- If macOS prompts on first launch on another machine, right-click the app and choose Open.
